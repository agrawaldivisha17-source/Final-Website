Nutrition Tips

Nutrition is one of the most important parts of our health and development, because it provides us with the essential nutrients, vitamins, and proteins that we need to be healthy and grow normally. However, due to stress, many people stop taking care of their nutrition. This page will provide you with insights on how you can remain healthy when you do not have time to prepare meals/are stressed.

Tips

Drink water (at least 1-2 liters a day)
Eat protein (add protein and carbs to your meal so you are more full and energized throughout the day)
Eat fruits (fruits are a very healthy snack you can munch on instead of junk foods)
Don’t skip meals (Add reminders throughout the day to eat your meals, or meal-prep!)
